📅 Published: 8 March 2026

Welcome to Anywhere Appliance - where convenience meets quality and that is right at your doorstep.

We are a Kolkata based online store that is committed to bringing you the best range of portable and compact home appliances that will fit into your lifestyle - whether at home, outdoors, on the go, or living in a small space.

Who We Are?

At Anywhere Appliance, we believe that great appliances shouldn't be limited in size and location. From your kitchen counter to your campsite, from your apartment balcony to your backyard - we bring you smart portable solutions that work anywhere you need it to.

Based out of Maheshtala, Kolkata, West Bengal, we are a locally based business with a passion for making everyday life more convenient and enjoyable for households around India.

What We Offer?

We carefully curate a wide range of portable appliances that fall into many categories:

Kitchen and Cooking Appliances

Portable stoves, espresso makers & ice makers.

Grills and Outdoor Cooking Equipment

BBQ grills and gas grills.

Cleaning Equipment

Steam cleaners, vacuum cleaners, carpet and upholstery cleaners.

Laundry and Dishwashing Solutions

Portable washing machines, washer-dryer combos, and countertop dishwashers.

Every single product in our store is chosen with a single purpose in mind, which is to make your life easier, and that no matter where you are.
