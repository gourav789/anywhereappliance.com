At Anywhere Appliance, we take the privacy of our customers, as well as our website visitors very seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information when you visit our website at anywhereappliance.com or order something from us. By using our website you agree to the terms states in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you browse our website or place an order with us we may gather certain personal information from you. This includes your name, delivery address, email address and details of your order. We gather this information only when you voluntarily provide it to us - for instance through making an order or contacting us by email. We may also automatically collect non-personal information such as the type of browser you are using, device type, IP address, pages visited and time spent on our website. This information is gathered via standard Web analytics software and helps us to understand how our site is being used so that we can enhance your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We only use the information we collect for the following purposes: to process and fulfill your orders, to communicate with you about the status of your order or to answer your questions or address your concerns when you contact us. We do not use your personal information for unsolicited marketing and promotional purposes without your consent.

Cash on Delivery and Payment

Anywhere Appliance accepts Cash on Delivery and it is the only mode of payment. As a result, we do not collect, store, or process any credit card, debit card or on-line banking information whatsoever. Your financial information is always kept to yourself.

Advertising and Google AdSense

Our website uses Google AdSense advertising service provided by Google llc for displaying advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and other similar tracking technologies to deliver to you ads that are relevant to you on the basis of your browsing history and your interest. These are called interest-based advertisements or personalized advertisements.

When you visit our website, Google can collect information such as your IP address, the type of web browser you are using, the web pages you visit, and your interactions with advertisements. This information is then used by Google to show you ads which may be of interest to you across the websites that participate in the Google AdSense network.

Google's use of advertising cookies is to facilitate the delivery of advertising by Google and its advertising associates to you based upon your visit to our site and the sites across the internet. You may disable personalized advertising by going to Google's Ads Settings g.co/adsettings. Alternatively, you can opt out of personalized advertising cookies from third-party vendors that might track you by visiting www.aboutads.info.

We do not have direct access to the data collected by Google AdSense, and we have no control over the content of the advertisements which are to be displayed on our website. Google runs its own advertising privacy practices, and you can ask Google for details on the specific use of your data and connection to advertising by using Google's Privacy Policy, policies.google.com/privacy.

Please note that advertisers and ad networks such as Google, third parties, may also use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to measure the effectiveness of their advertising and/or to personalize the ad content you see. We have no access to these third-party tracking technologies, nor control over them.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, rent or otherwise share your personal information with 3rd parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes. We will be sharing your delivery information only with trusted logistics or courier partners so that they can deliver your order to you. These partners are bound to securely manage your information and are not allowed to use it for any other purpose.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification or destruction. However, please note that no means of data transmission over the internet and no method of electronic storage is wholly secure. While we strive for the use of commercially acceptable means to guard your personal data, we can not guarantee that your personal data is absolutely secure.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to give you a better experience browsing and to use an advertising system (Google AdSense). Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device and help us to understand how visitors interact with our website, to enhance the functionality of our sites and to enable our advertising partners to provide relevant advertising. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings; however doing so may affect certain features of our website and the relevance of advertisements that you see. You can also control your cookie preference and opt out of personalised advertising, through Google's Ads Settings or via the privacy settings in your browser.

Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third party Web sites or resources. Please note that after you exit our website we have no control over the content of, nor the privacy practices of, those outside websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites that you visit.

Children's Privacy

Our website and its services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly take personal information from children. If you think a child has shared with us any personal information, please contact us and we will consider taking measures to remove such information as soon as possible.

Your Rights

You have a right to access, correct or request deletion of the personal information that we hold about you. If you want to exercise any of these rights or have any concerns about how your data is being used then please contact us at the details provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect a change in our practice or for legal and regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date on the top. We encourage you to check this page on a periodic basis to be informed about how we are making sure your information is safe.
